{% trans "Najot Ta'lim Feedback" %}

{% blocktrans %} The Najot Ta'lim Feedback Site is designed to collect and analyze feedback on services, classes, and overall experiences at the Najot Ta'lim educational center. The site allows students and their parents to identify strengths and areas for improvement at the center. Feedback is gathered through an online form, enhancing transparency and openness. The collected feedback is used to improve services and introduce new opportunities. The site also showcases the center’s achievements and successes, providing inspiration and strengthening its image. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans '1. Question: How can I submit feedback?' %}

{% blocktrans %} You can submit feedback using the online form available on our site. Simply fill out the required sections and submit your comments. Anonymity is an option if you prefer. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans '2. Question: How is feedback processed and what results are published?' %}

{% blocktrans %} Feedback is regularly analyzed. Monthly or quarterly reports summarizing general trends and improvements are published on our site. Individual feedback responses are not provided, but general responses and discussion outcomes are shared. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans '3. Question: Is my feedback anonymous?' %}

{% blocktrans %} Yes, you can submit feedback anonymously. Your personal information will not be disclosed, but your feedback will be used for general analysis. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans '4. Question: Can I participate in discussions on the site?' %}

{% blocktrans %} Yes, the site includes a discussion forum where you can engage with other users. You can exchange opinions, discuss feedback, and participate in conversations on various topics. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans '5. Question: What topics can be discussed in the forums?' %}

{% blocktrans %} The forums cover topics such as services, educational processes, teachers, classes, and other aspects of the educational center’s activities. You can share your opinions and engage in discussions with other users on these topics. {% endblocktrans %}
